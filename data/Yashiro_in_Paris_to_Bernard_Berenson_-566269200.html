Paris
(Chez Monsieur Hagiwara
7 Av. Hoche, Paris 8e)

Jan 22

Dear B.B.,

I take the liberty to introduce the bearer of this letter Mr. Atsuo Imaizumi, a well-recognized art-critic of modern art in Japan, a sort of pupil of mine, who is visiting Italy. At the same time He is an intimate friend & colleague of Miwa, who is translating your book into Japanese. If you can meet him & give him a chance to see your collection & library, I shall appreciate it as a great education to Mr. Imaizumi.

Yours ever,

Yukio Yashiro
