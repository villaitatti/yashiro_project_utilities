Settignano. Dec. 30. 55

Dear Yashiro,

Let me quote what Walker writes me about your work on Far Eastern Art. 

“If Yashiro will send me the outline of his work, and his own biographical data, I shall go to work with the Bollinger Foundation.”

I hope this will be forwarded if you already have left for Italy.

With my good wish for 1956.

Affectionately,

B.B.
