Settignano. July 4. 55

My dear Yashiro,

Thanks for Etudes sur les Problems Sti [Stylistiques?]. I fear you have gone to great expense of time and money to procure it. I am sending you a bibliography that has just appeared of my publications.

In no distant future I hope to send the English version of “Lorenzo Lotto,” the first part of the catalogue of Italian Painting, and some smaller things.

Do go on with your book on Far Eastern Art. I am sure you will find a publisher.

Affectionately,

B.B.
