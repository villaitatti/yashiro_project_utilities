Sept. 5. 56
Casa al Dono
Vallombrosa
(Prov. Di Firenze)

Dear Yashiro,                              

This will introduce my very dear friends Mr. and Mrs. Charles Wrightsman of New York. Show them all of the best in Japan that they can see in a short stay: I shall be very grateful. Johnnie Walker was here for a week, and we discussed your problems, and mean to do all we can to help you out in America and elsewhere.

Remember me most respectfully to your wife.

Affectionately,

B.B.
